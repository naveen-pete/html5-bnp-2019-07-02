Man from Peru

There once was a man from Peru, Who had a lot of growing up to do. He'd ring a doorbell, then run like hell, Until the owner shot him with a .22

Fellow named Gus

There was an odd fellow named Gus, When travelling he made such a fuss. He was banned from the train, Not allowed on a plane, And now travels only by bus.

Farmer from Leeds

There once was a farmer from Leeds, Who swallowed a packet of seeds. It soon came to pass, He was covered with grass, But has all the tomatoes he needs.

Woman named Bright

There was a young woman named Bright, Whose speed was much faster than light. She set out one day, In a relative way, And returned on the previous night.

Man from Tibet

There once was a man from Tibet, Who couldn't find a cigarette. So he smoked all his socks, and got chicken-pocks, and had to go to the vet.

Child in Spain

There once was a child in Spain, Who loved to play in the rain. One day he tripped, And broke his hip, Now he is in serious pain.
