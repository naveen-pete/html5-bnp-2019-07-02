The background-position property

By default, the first tile's top-left coordinates corresponds to the padding box top-left coordinates. However the top-left of the first tile can be shifted using background-position property. It takes both absolute units for offsets (px) and relative (% with respect to content width of element). With relative offsets (%) however, the center of the background image is positioned by the given values. Thus 50% horizontal and vertical offsets actually center the background image.
