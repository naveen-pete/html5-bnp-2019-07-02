The background-size property

The background-size property is used to resize the background image. The size can be specified using absolute (px) or relative (%) units. It also take 2 special values cover and contain. Both cover and contain maintain the aspect ratio of the image (ratio of width to height) - while cover expands the image till both right and bottom borders are hit (possibly cropping the image right or bottom portion), contain resizes the image till one of the borders - right or bottom, is hit.
