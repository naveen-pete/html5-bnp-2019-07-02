The HSL Model

Colors can also be represented as a mixture of hue, saturation and lightness values. Hue is the base color and is represented as a degree along a "color wheel". Saturation, represented as a percentage value, is the extent of the hue, and lightness, again represented as a percentage, is the degree of whiteness/darkness (50% is the hue, saturation exactly as it is chosen). Unlike the rgb model, the hsl model does not provide a unique representaion for a color.

Values specified using hsl() (h -> degree between 0 - 360; s -> 0% - 100%; l -> 0% (black) - 100% (white))
