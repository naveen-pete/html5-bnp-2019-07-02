Box Model - The Margin Area

The margin area is the space between 2 elements. The margin on all directions can be adjusted individually. Margins can be specified in absolute (eg. px) or relative (eg. %) units. Margin has longhand properties and a shorthand (margin). Horizontal margins "add up", whereas vertical margins "collapse". However collapsing happens only in a block formatting context.
